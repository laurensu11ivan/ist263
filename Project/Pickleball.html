Objective of the Game:

  • smack a ball over the net until they win the point when the opponent cannot return it
  • Pickleball is a sport that combines tennis, badminton, and ping-pong. It is plated with a paddle and a plastic ball with holes that looks similar to a wiffle ball. It is played indoor or outdoor, singles or doubles and guarantees a game of fun!
  • The court is a tennis court with a no volley vote that is 7’ behind the net. This is also called the “kitchen”.

Background:

  • Pickleball was invented in 1965 on Bainbridge Island, a short ferry ride from Seattle, Washington. Three dads – Joel Pritchard, Bill Bell, and Barney McCallum who made it to keep their children occupied over the summer are credited for creating game. Pickleball has evolved from original handmade equipment and simple rules into a popular sport throughout the US and Canada. The game is growing internationally as well, with many European and Asian countries adding courts.
  • In 2003 there were 39 known places to play in North America listed on the Pickleball stuff website. These were in 10 states, and 150 individual courts. In 2015 there was 12,800 nationwide courts to play on. In 2019 it was declared the fastest growing sports in the U.S as participants reached 3.3 million. In 2021, it grew to 4.8 million players in the US.
